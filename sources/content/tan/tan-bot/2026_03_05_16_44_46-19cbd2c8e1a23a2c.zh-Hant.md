Dear all,

The NCTS Theoretical and Computational Astrophysics (TCA)
<https://nctstca.github.io/index.html> is happy to announce the 2026
undergraduate summer student program (SSP)
<https://nctstca.github.io/events/202607-tcassp/>. The SSP aims to bring
research experiences in theoretical and computational astrophysics to
undergraduate students, featuring:
(1) One 3-day workshop on July/01-03 (Wed-Fri) at NTU
(2) A two-month (01 July – 31 Aug) full-time research program
(3) Final oral presentation on Aug/27-27 (Thu-Fri) at NTHU

Please encourage your students to apply via the website by *April/10th*.
Please also kindly help to forward this message to anyone who may be
interested.

You can find the poster attached and see the website for further
information: https://nctstca.github.io/events/202607-tcassp/

Best regards,
Yueh-Ning, on behave of the NCTS-TCA SSP Committee
