Dear all,

There are two News items uploaded to ALMA Science Portal. 

(1) Web-based OT for the Cycle 13 Call and Expected Capabilities for Cycle 14
https://almascience.nao.ac.jp/news/web-based-ot-for-cycle-13-call-and-expected-capabilities-during-cycle-14
This announcement includes:
    (i) the replacement of the desktop-based OT with the Web-based OT (ngOT), and
    (ii) the announcement of the expected capabilities for Cycle 14: The anticipated top-level capabilities, including the available observing hours and the configuration schedule, remain unchanged

(2) Release of ALMA Band 2 Science Verification Data and Announcement of Intent to Release Arp 220 Data
https://almascience.nao.ac.jp/news/new-release-of-alma-science-verification-data-

This announcement includes the release of ALMA Band 2 Science Verification data for three targets:
   - G31.41+0.31 (spectral scan)
   - SPT 0027-50 (spectral scan)
   - HR 5907 (full polarization)
It also includes a pre-announcement of the upcoming release of data for Arp 220.
