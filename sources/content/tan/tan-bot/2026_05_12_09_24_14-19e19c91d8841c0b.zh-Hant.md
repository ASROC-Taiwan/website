> RATIONALE:
>
> The East Asian VLBI Workshop (EAVW) is an annual international meeting
> that promotes collaboration among VLBI communities in East Asia and
beyond.
> The workshop series began in 2008 and has since been hosted in various
> countries across the region, strengthening scientific and technical
cooperation
> among research institutes and universities.
> A major milestone in regional VLBI collaboration was the establishment of
> the East Asian VLBI Network (EAVN) in 2018. Formed through cooperation
> among institutes in China, Japan and Korea, EAVN integrates major
facilities
> such as KVN, VERA, and the Chinese VLBI stations, significantly enhancing
> high-resolution radio astronomy capabilities in East Asia.
> EAVW has played an important role in fostering this collaboration by
providing
> a platform to share scientific results, discuss technological
developments, and
> explore future directions for VLBI research.
> EAVW2026 continues this tradition in Fukui, Japan.
