Dear Researchers,

This is a call for applications for the 2026 NCTS Postdoc Paper Award:
https://www.phys.ncts.ntu.edu.tw/en/program/postdoc_paper_award

The NCTS Postdoc Paper Award is to encourage theoretical physics
postdoctoral researchers to conduct research on important subjects and to
carry out influential research works.

Published work between January 1, 2025 and December 31, 2025 will be
considered.

To apply, please refer to the "NCTS Postdoc Paper Award 2026" page below,
complete the "Sign Up" section first, and proceed to the "Submission" page
to upload the following 4 required documents:
1. Application Form (DOC)
2. CV (PDF)
3. Publication List (PDF)
4. One Representative Paper (PDF)

NCTS Postdoc Paper Award 2026:
https://www.phys.ncts.ntu.edu.tw/act/actnews/NCTS-Postdoc-Paper-Award-2026-73051870?method=show_data

*Application Deadline: Monday, April 20, 2026*
