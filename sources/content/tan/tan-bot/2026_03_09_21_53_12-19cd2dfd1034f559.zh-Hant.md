Dear Colleagues,

Sorry for the delay. We are pleased to announce the Call for Proposals for
the LOT 2026B semester (May 1–August 31, 2026). Attached are the detailed
announcement and the LaTeX proposal templates. Please read the announcement
carefully, as it is updated regularly, for example, for proposal(s)
re-submitted from past semesters, please address TAC comments in Section
15. Proposal(s) that do not show improvement will be rejected.

Submission deadline: March 31, 2026 at 23:59 (Taiwan local time)

Please submit your proposal as a single PDF, including the compiled
proposal and one page for figures and/or tables, by email to me with the
subject line “LOT 2026B proposal.” If you have any questions, feel free to
contact me.

Thank you for your collaboration!

Best regards,
Janet Chen (NCU) on behalf of LOT TAC
