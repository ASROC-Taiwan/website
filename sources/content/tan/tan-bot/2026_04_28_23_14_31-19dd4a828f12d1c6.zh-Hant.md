> Hello,
> My name is Boris Wilmart, I am a French documentary filmmaker specialising in astronomy and our relationship with the cosmos. I found your contact through the IAU National Outreach Coordinators website.
> I am reaching out because I am currently working on a participatory film project called Ubuntu.
> The idea is to invite people from all over the world to film one or more short videos answering this question:
> "If you could send a message into space, to another form of life — what would you say? What would you show of yourself and of life on Earth?"
> These videos will then be assembled to form a collective message from humanity to the universe. The project is inspired in part by the Voyager Golden Record — though here the message is symbolic. It is really about reflecting on what it means to be human, our place on Earth, and what connects us all.
> Your engagement with astronomy seemed to me naturally in tune with the spirit of this project, which is why I wanted to reach out. If you are interested, you could:
>
> • participate yourself, by filming your own message;
> • or simply share this call for contributions with your community.
>
> Everyone is free to film however they wish — themselves, their surroundings, a moment from daily life — in their own language, with their own means. Any participation, in whatever form, will be invaluable.
