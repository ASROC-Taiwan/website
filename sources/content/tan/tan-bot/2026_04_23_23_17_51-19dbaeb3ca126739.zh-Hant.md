Dear all,

The 2026 Scientific Assembly of the Astronomical Society of the Republic of
China (ASROC2026) will be held at the College of Hakka Studies at National
Yang Ming Chiao Tung University in Zhubei on May 15 (Friday) – May 17
(Sunday), 2026. The meeting is approaching soon, and we would like to
remind all participants that the online registration deadline is April 26,
2026. Please complete your registration as soon as possible if you have not
done so.

The scientific program has now been announced and is available on the
ASROC2026 website https://indico.phys.nthu.edu.tw/e/asroc2026.

We look forward to seeing you in Zhubei in May!
