Dear Colleagues,

We are pleased to announce the Call for Proposals for the LOT 2026A
semester (January 1–April 30, 2026). Attached are the detailed announcement
and the LaTeX proposal templates. Please read the announcement carefully,
as it is updated regularly.

Submission deadline: November 30, 2025 at 23:59 (Taiwan local time)

Please submit your proposal as a single PDF, including the compiled
proposal and one page for figures and/or tables, by email to me with the
subject line “LOT 2026A proposal.” If you have any questions, feel free to
contact me.

Thank you for your collaboration!

Best regards,
Janet Chen (NCU) on behalf of LOT TAC
