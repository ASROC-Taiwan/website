## ASROC 2025 Annual Meeting

The 2025 Scientific Assembly of the Astronomical Society of the Republic of China (ASROC2025) will be held at the National Formosa University on May 16 (Friday) â€“ May 18 (Sunday). The official website for the 2025 ASROC Annual Meeting is now live, and registration is open. Those interested in attending are encouraged to register through the ASROC website.
